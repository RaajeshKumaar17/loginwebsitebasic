COIR PRODUCTS

                    Coir products are natural and sustainable, crafted from coconut husk fibers. These eco-friendly items are widely used for their durability and 
                    biodegradable nature.Coir products include mats, rugs, ropes, brushes, mattresses, and garden materials. They are valued for their  strength, 
                    water resistance, and versatility.Coir mats and rugs are perfect for adding an earthy touch to homes and offices. Coir ropes are extensively 
                    used in agriculture and construction due to their robustness. Brushes made from coir are durable and ideal for cleaning purposes Coir mattresses
                    provide excellent comfort and natural support for a healthy sleep.In gardening, coir is used in pots, planters, and erosion control mats to 
                    promote plant growth Coir geotextiles are effective in controlling soil erosion and are environmentally safe. Coir products are popular for 
                    their sustainable production process, reducing waste and promoting green living.
